Refund Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Refund Window

Customers can request a refund within 14 days of the original transaction date. For subscription renewals, customers can request a refund within 14 days of the renewal date.

2. How Refunds Are Processed

Payments are processed by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Refund processing follows Paddle buyer terms and is handled through Paddle's billing support channels.

3. Subscription Cancellation

Subscriptions renew monthly unless canceled. You can cancel renewal at any time through your billing portal. Canceling prevents future renewals and does not affect already delivered service in the current billing period.

4. Contact

For refund or billing support, contact support@reqmd.com with your order information.
